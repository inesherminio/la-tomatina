La Tomatina

How to play

Every year in Pamplona, Spain, people come together to party, throwing tomatoes at each other, staining as many festivaleros as possible. Your goal is to hit with tomatoes every festivalero that comes in your way. If one of them reaches your side of the battlefield you lose the game. It is time for you to join La Tomatina!
