La Tomatina

Story

Every year in Pamplona, Spain, people come together to party, throwing tomatoes at each other. It is time for you to join La Tomatina!

The goal

Your goal is to hit with tomatoes every festivalero that comes in your way and not get hit!

You lose when

  • More than 3 festivaleros reach your side of the battlefield without being hit
  • You get hit more than twice
  • You splash a tomato into a clotheslines
